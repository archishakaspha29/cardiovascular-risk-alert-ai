Notes:
The model showed very high performance on the available public test datasets. These results should be interpreted carefully because the risk label was engineered using clinically relevant health indicators, and the model may be learning patterns that are closely tied to those label rules.

These results are based on public datasets and should not be interpreted as clinical validation.

Limitations:
- The model has not been clinically validated.
- The high accuracy may partly reflect the way the risk label was engineered.
- Dataset representation may be limited.
- Results may not generalize to all populations.
- This tool is for risk awareness and early care-seeking support only, not medical diagnosis.

Future Work:
